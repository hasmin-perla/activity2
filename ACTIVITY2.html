RAINBOW COLORS

Red It symbolizes energy, passion, and love. It is a color that evokes strong emotions.
Orange It represents enthusiasm, creativity, and warmth, combining red and yellow. It is associated with joy and positivity.
Yellow It represents happiness, positivity, and intellect. It is a bright and uplifting color, associated with sunshine and warmth.
Green It symbolizes growth, harmony, and renewal. It is associated with tranquility and balance.
BlueIt represents calmness, stability, and trust. It is associated with the sky and the ocean, symbolizing serenity.
Indigo It symbolizes intuition, spirituality, and wisdom. It is a deep and mystical color.
Violet It represents creativity, imagination, and spirituality. It is associated with royalty and luxury.
